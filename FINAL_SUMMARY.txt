📡 API ENDPOINTS READY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Customers:     GET/POST /api/customers
               GET/PUT/DELETE /api/customers/:id

Calls:         POST /api/calls/trigger         (Main endpoint)
               GET /api/calls
               GET /api/calls/:id
               GET /api/calls/:id/status
               GET /api/calls/:id/transcript
               GET /api/calls/:id/recording
               POST /api/calls/:id/transfer
               GET /api/calls/stats/summary

System:        GET /api/health                 (Health check)
               POST /webhooks/bolna            (Bolna webhooks)
